Our Cleaning Services

Basic Cleaning

Our Basic Cleaning service is designed to keep your home or office fresh, tidy, and welcoming. We cover all essential tasks like dusting, sweeping, vacuuming, mopping, and disinfecting high-touch surfaces. Perfect for regular upkeep, this service focuses on maintaining a clean and healthy space without the deep detail of a full deep clean.

  • Dusting all surfaces
  • Vacuuming and mopping floors
  • Cleaning countertops & tabletops
  • Sanitizing kitchen & bathroom surfaces
  • Taking out trash and replacing liners
  • Wiping mirrors and glass surfaces

Deep Cleaning

Our Deep Cleaning service is designed for homes or offices that need extra attention and a full refresh from top to bottom. This service includes everything in our Basic Cleaning, plus detailed work in hard-to-reach areas and high-traffic zones. We scrub, sanitize, and polish every surface to eliminate built-up dust, grime, and bacteria; leaving your space feeling brand new.

  • Sofa vacuuming
  • Dust lamps, vents, and fan blades
  • Under carpet sweep
  • Clean/polish under and behind furniture
  • Dust shelves and cabinets
  • Take care of dirty dishes
  • Windows, doors, and baseboard cleaning
  • Mini bar areas

Move-In / Move-Out

Full cleaning of empty properties—ready for new tenants or owners.
