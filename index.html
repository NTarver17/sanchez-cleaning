About Sandra Sanchez

Hi, I’m the founder of Sanchez’s Cleaning Services. I came to the United States from Guatemala with the dream of building a better future for my family through honest, hard work. I’m a proud and loving mother of three, and everything I do—including this business—is built on love, dedication, and resilience.

With over 15 years of hands-on experience in residential and office cleaning, I’ve built a reputation for doing more than just cleaning spaces — I care for them. Every home or office I walk into is treated with the same respect and attention I would give my own.

What sets my service apart is not just the spotless results, but the heart behind it. I always put my full effort and love into every job, no matter how big or small, because I genuinely care that my clients feel peace and satisfaction in their space.
